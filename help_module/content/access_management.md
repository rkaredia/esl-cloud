# Access Management

Manage users and their permissions within your organization.

## Roles
- **Global Admin**: Full access to all companies and stores.
- **Company Owner**: Can manage all stores within their company and create staff/managers.
- **Store Manager**: Can manage assigned stores, products, and tags.
- **Store Staff**: Can view data and perform basic operations like tag pairing.
- **Read-Only**: Can view data but cannot make changes.

## Store Assignment
Users must be assigned to specific stores to see data for those stores. This ensures that a manager at Store A cannot accidentally change prices for Store B.
