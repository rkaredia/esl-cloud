# Getting Started with SAIS ESL

Welcome to the SAIS Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) Management Platform. This guide will help you understand the basic workflow to get your digital labels up and running.

## System Overview

The SAIS platform allows you to manage digital price tags across your stores. The system consists of:
1. **Products**: Your store's inventory and pricing.
2. **ESL Tags**: The physical digital labels on your shelves.
3. **Gateways**: Communication hubs that send updates from the platform to the tags.

## The Basic Workflow

1. **Select Your Store**: Use the store selector at the top right to choose the store you are working in.
2. **Import Products**: Upload your price book (e.g., from Modisoft) to populate your product list.
3. **Register Tags**: Ensure your ESL tags are registered in the system and assigned to a gateway.
4. **Link Tags to Products**: Pair each physical tag with a specific product in the platform.
5. **Monitor Updates**: Use the Dashboard to ensure all tags are successfully updated.

## Key Navigation

- **Inventory**: Manage your Products, ESL Tags, and Suppliers.
- **Hardware**: Monitor Gateways and view technical specifications.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Check system health, battery levels, and update progress.
